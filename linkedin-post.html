Prayer Recovery App · PWA · Free Forever
🆕 LAUNCH
If you've ever felt the weight of missed prayers and didn't know where to start — this is for you. I built Qadaa, a free PWA that helps you make up missed prayers, one day at a time: 📿 Track your missed prayers — enter your years and get your total instantly. 🔥 Build a daily streak — consistency is the secret, don't break it. 🏆 Earn points & badges — real motivation for a long journey. 📊 See your progress — completion estimate, daily pace, weekly grid. No login. No data sent anywhere. Works fully offline. Built with React 19 + Vite 8 + Zustand + Tailwind v4 + PWA. The journey is long — but it starts with one prayer.
